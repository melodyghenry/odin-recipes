Southwest Skillet

Found on recipe runner

Description:

Big bean and potato skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups peeled and diced sweet potato
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsps chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 ounces dried green chiles
  • 1/2 cup salsa or salsa verde
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • lime juice
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar, colby or monterey jack
  • Additional toppings: avocado, sour cream, Greek yogurt

Directions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet. Add the diced sweet potatoes, salt and pepper, and saute on medium heat for about 8 minutes.
  2. Add 3-4 tablespoons of water and cover with a lid. Let the sweet potatoes steam until they're fork tender, about 4 minutes more.
  3. Add the green chiles, black beans, rice, spices, salsa, cilantro, lime juice, salt and pepper to the skiller. Stir everything to combine.
  4. Add the shredded cheese to top. Cover the skillet with a lid and cook for 3-4 minutes until the cheese is melted and everything is warm.
  5. Add desired toppings.
