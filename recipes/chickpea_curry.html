Coconut Curry Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1.5 tbsp curry powder
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 15-oz. cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1 8-oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1 13.5oz. can full-fat coconut milk
  • 4 oz. kale, chopped

Directions:

  1. Dice onion, mince garlic, grate ginger.
  2. Add the onion, garlic and giner to a large skillet with the olive oil. Saute over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes, or until the onions are soft and translucent.
  3. Add the curry powder and cumin. Continue to stir for one minute.
  4. Drain chickpeas, add to the skillet with tomato sauce and coconut milk. Stir to combine
  5. Turn up to medium-high to simmer. Once simmering, turn back down to medium-low to simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the kale by stirring it in. Cook until the kale wilts, about 5 minutes. Salt to taste. Serve with bread or rice.
