Ingredients for plain biscuits:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 6 tbsp. unsalted butter (freeze the butter overnight and grate it just before mixing)
  • 3/4 cup whole buttermilk (whole milk will also work)
For cheddar and japapeno biscuits add:
  • Cheddar (amount variable)
  • Jalapenos
