Cheddar and Jalapeno Biscuits

Description:

A flaky, savory biscuit, perfect for breakfast or a snack

Ingredients for plain biscuits:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 6 tbsp. unsalted buttexsr (freeze the butter overnight and grate it just before mixing)
  • 3/4 cup whole buttermilk (whole milk will also work)
  • Extra butter and flour for topping, surfaces etc.
For cheddar and japapeno biscuits add:
  • Cheddar (amount variable)
  • Jalapenos

Directions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients.
  2. Add the grated butter.
  3. Add the milk slowly, mixing gently with a spatula or flat instrument until crumbly but sticky.
  4. Place the dough on a non-stick surface, using extra flour on the surface.
  5. Flatten the dough into an 8-inch square. Fold into two by hand quickly four times.
  6. On the last fold, if using, add the grated cheese, jalapeno or other toppings.
  7. Top the biscuits with butter (mayonnaise will also work).
  8. Bake for 17-20 minutes.
